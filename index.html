CLCNet - Audio Samples

Audio samples accompanying the paper CLCNet: Deep learning-based noise reduction for hearing aids using complex linear coding. All samples are from the test set. The maximum attenuation of both Wiener Filter and Complex Linear Coding is limited to 14dB.
